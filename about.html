Mission

Our mission at the University of Birmingham Dubai is to deliver world-class education, research, and innovation that benefits society locally, nationally, and globally. We strive to foster a diverse and inclusive community of students, faculty, and staff who are engaged in advancing knowledge and making a positive impact on the world.

Values

  • Excellence: We are committed to academic excellence in teaching, research, and innovation.
  • Integrity: We uphold the highest ethical standards in all aspects of our work.
  • Diversity and Inclusion: We celebrate diversity and promote an inclusive environment where all individuals are valued and respected.
  • Community Engagement: We engage with our local and global communities to address societal challenges and contribute to positive change.
  • Sustainability: We are dedicated to promoting environmental sustainability and responsible stewardship of resources.
