Programs - University of Birmingham Dubai

Bachelor of Science in Computer Science

The Bachelor of Science in Computer Science program at the University of Birmingham Dubai provides students with a comprehensive understanding of computer science principles and their applications in various fields. Students will learn programming languages, algorithms, data structures, software engineering, and more.

Requirements: High school diploma or equivalent

Prerequisites: Mathematics, Physics

Bachelor of Business Administration

The Bachelor of Business Administration program at the University of Birmingham Dubai offers students a broad understanding of business principles and practices. Students will study areas such as accounting, finance, marketing, management, and business law, preparing them for diverse career opportunities in the business world.

Requirements: High school diploma or equivalent

Prerequisites: None

Bachelor of Arts in English Literature

The Bachelor of Arts in English Literature program at the University of Birmingham Dubai focuses on the study of literary texts from various periods and cultures. Students will analyze novels, plays, poetry, and other forms of literature, developing critical thinking and communication skills.

Requirements: High school diploma or equivalent

Prerequisites: English Language

Bachelor of Engineering in Civil Engineering

The Bachelor of Engineering in Civil Engineering program at the University of Birmingham Dubai provides students with a comprehensive understanding of civil engineering principles and their applications in various infrastructure projects. Students will study structural engineering, transportation engineering, environmental engineering, and more.

Requirements: High school diploma or equivalent

Prerequisites: Mathematics, Physics
